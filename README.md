# Sudoku (Ncurses)

Sudoku is played by arranging numbers 1-9 on a 9x9 game board so that each row,
column, and 3x3 box only has the numbers 1-9 once.
For example:
```
1 3 7 | 6 9 2 | 8 5 4
4 9 2 | 7 8 5 | 6 1 3
6 5 8 | 4 1 3 | 2 7 9
- - -   - - -   - - -
3 6 1 | 9 2 4 | 5 8 7
2 8 9 | 1 5 7 | 3 4 6
7 4 5 | 8 3 6 | 9 2 1
- - -   - - -   - - -
5 2 6 | 3 7 1 | 4 9 8
9 7 3 | 2 4 8 | 1 6 5
8 1 4 | 5 6 9 | 7 3 2
```
